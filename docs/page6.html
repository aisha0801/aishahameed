About Me


I am a recent graduate from the University of Texas at Dallas with a B.S. in Information Technology and Systems. I am passionate about data analysis and define myself as someone who has a drive for growth whilst looking for an opportunity to make meaningful contributions and develop myself professionally.

I recently completed an internship at Epsilon where I focused on data center management. I performed data center audits, validated inventory databases, and supported the technical team with rack and stack server operations. Additionally, I worked on a team to create a MySQL database schema with a user-friendly GUI to streamline inventory management. This hands-on experience has solidified my interest in data analysis and has equipped me with practical skills in managing data.

Furthermore I have also undertaken various academic and personal projects to further enhance my skills. These include cleaning and manipulating datasets, and creating analytical and descriptive visualizations, which have provided me with valuable hands-on experience.

Originally from New York, I am a family-oriented book-lover, avid baker, cat and café enthusiast.
